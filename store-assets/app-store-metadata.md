### Description
> Up to 4,000 characters. Benefit-led — lead with what users feel, not what the app contains.

```
Growing up is hard. Some days feel heavy, overwhelming, or just off — and that's okay. Authentic Steps gives you a quiet corner of your day to breathe, reflect, and remember who you are.

This is your daily ritual: three minutes of gratitude, a small intention to carry through your day, and an "I am" statement that grounds you in your own worth. It's not about being perfect. It's about showing up for yourself, one step at a time.

─────────────────────────────
WHAT'S INSIDE
─────────────────────────────

🌱 Daily Ritual
A guided three-part practice — gratitude, intention, and affirmation — designed to take just a few minutes each morning. Complete it every day to build your streak and earn milestones that mark how far you've come.

🌬️ Breathing Exercises
Feeling anxious or overwhelmed? Box breathing and guided breathwork exercises help you slow down and find your centre in under five minutes. Available any time from the Toolbox.

🖐️ Grounding — 5-4-3-2-1
When your thoughts are racing, the 5-4-3-2-1 grounding technique brings you back to the present moment using your five senses. A simple, proven exercise you can do anywhere.

📓 Journal
Your entries are yours. Look back at your gratitudes, intentions, and reflections across the past 90 days. Export a personal journal PDF whenever you want a record to keep.

🏅 Streaks & Milestones
A daily streak tracks your consistency. Streak freezes give you grace on tough days without losing your progress. Milestones celebrate the moments that matter — your first ritual, your first week, your first month.

─────────────────────────────
BUILT FOR YOUNG PEOPLE
─────────────────────────────

Authentic Steps was created specifically for young people navigating the pressures of school, relationships, identity, and everything in between. The language is warm, not clinical. The practices are short enough to actually do. And your data belongs to you — stored securely on your account.

No ads. No social features. No pressure.

─────────────────────────────
PRIVACY & SAFETY
─────────────────────────────

Your journal entries and personal data are stored privately on your account. We do not sell data, show advertisements, or share your information with third parties. For full details, see our Privacy Policy.

Sign up takes less than a minute. No invite required.
```
